Privacy Policy

Welcome to AKROS (also known as "NeuroForge"), a cognitive training application developed and operated by the individual developer trading as MindyLabs ("we", "us", "our").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the AKROS mobile application ("App", "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. We operate from India.

By using AKROS, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you set up AKROS, we collect:

  • Operator Name (optional): A display name you choose for personalization within the App
  • Training Preferences: Your selected training goals, experience level, and session duration preferences
  • Notification Preferences: Your chosen time for daily training reminders

We do NOT collect:

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Date of birth
  • Gender or demographic information
  • Location data
  • Payment card information (handled securely by Google Play)

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

Training Performance Data (stored locally on your device):

  • Cognitive module session results (reaction time, accuracy, consistency metrics)
  • Training load levels and adaptive difficulty adjustments
  • Baseline assessment scores
  • Training streaks and session history
  • Performance trends over time

Device Information:

  • We do NOT collect device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, Advertising ID)
  • We do NOT track your device serial number
  • We do NOT collect IP addresses

Usage Data:

  • We do NOT use analytics services (no Firebase, Google Analytics, or third-party trackers)
  • We do NOT log your activity outside the App
  • We do NOT track your behavior

1.3 Subscription Information

If you purchase a PRO subscription:

  • Purchase verification is handled entirely by Google Play Billing
  • We receive a purchase token to verify your subscription status
  • Payment information (credit card, billing address) is processed exclusively by Google Play and NEVER shared with us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for the following purposes:

Essential App Functions

  • Personalization: Display your chosen operator name in the App interface
  • Adaptive Training: Adjust cognitive load based on your performance metrics
  • Progress Tracking: Show your training history, streaks, and performance trends
  • Notifications: Send daily training reminders at your chosen time (if enabled)
  • Subscription Management: Verify your PRO tier status to unlock advanced modules

What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ Sell or share your data with third parties
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • ❌ Analyze your data for research purposes (without explicit consent)
  • ❌ Track your activity across other apps or websites
  • ❌ Send your data to external servers or cloud services

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Only

All your personal and training data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT upload your data to external servers, cloud storage, or remote databases.

3.2 Encryption

We take security seriously:

  • Database Encryption: All training performance data is encrypted using SQLCipher with AES-256 encryption
  • Sensitive Settings: Your subscription tier and database encryption keys are protected with Android Keystore and AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Secure Communication: All communication with Google Play Billing uses HTTPS encryption

3.3 Security Measures

  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not integrate analytics SDKs that could leak your data
  • No Crash Reporting: We do not use third-party crash reporting services
  • Secure Random Generation: We use cryptographically secure random number generators for all training algorithms
  • Code Obfuscation: Release builds use ProGuard to protect against reverse engineering
  • No Cloud Backup: Automatic backups to Google Cloud are disabled by default to prevent data leakage

3.4 Data Protection Limitations

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for: keeping your device physically secure, using a strong device lock screen password/PIN, and not rooting your device (which can bypass security protections).

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

4.2 Google Play Billing (Required for PRO Subscriptions)

If you purchase a PRO subscription, Google Play Billing processes your payment securely. Google receives your payment information, purchase token, and subscription status. We only receive a purchase verification token. Google's Privacy Policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 System Services (Android OS)

The App uses the following Android system services: AlarmManager, NotificationManager, Vibration Service. These services operate locally on your device and do NOT transmit data externally.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or subpoena, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or the public.

5. Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data. You can view your data, modify preferences, disable notifications, and delete your training data. Because your data is stored exclusively on your device and not on any remote servers we control, to exercise your right to erasure, simply uninstall the application. If you are located in the EU (GDPR), California (CCPA), or India (DPDPA), you have extensive specific rights. To exercise these rights, contact us at operator@mindylabs.xyz.

6. Children's Privacy

AKROS is NOT intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Retention

We retain your training data, baseline assessments, and preferences indefinitely locally on your device until you uninstall the App or manually delete it. This indefinite retention allows you to track cognitive improvement over months or years and benefit from adaptive algorithms.

8. International Data Transfers

AKROS stores all data locally on your device. We do NOT transfer your data internationally or to external servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
