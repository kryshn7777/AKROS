Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: FEBRUARY 19, 2026
SUMMARY

AKROS collects minimal data required to deliver cognitive training. We do not sell your data. All session data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Account information: name (optional), email address (for Pro/Institutional tiers)
  • Goal tier preference and session duration settings
  • Payment information (processed by our payment provider; we never store card details)

1.2 Data Generated Automatically

  • Cognitive performance metrics: reaction times, accuracy scores, d-prime values, load indices
  • Session metadata: duration, module type, timestamps, streak data
  • Device information: Android version, display refresh rate (for frame-accurate stimulus delivery)
  • App usage analytics: module launch frequency, session completion rates

1.3 Data We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Microphone, camera, or biometric data
  • Browsing history or data from other applications

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively for:

  • Training delivery — Adaptive algorithms require performance history to calibrate difficulty levels
  • Analytics — Performance trends, cognitive load calculation, fatigue index, and streak tracking
  • Service improvement — Aggregated, anonymized analytics to improve module design and staircase algorithms
  • Account management — Subscription status, tier access, and notifications you've opted into

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Local-First Architecture

All cognitive performance data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. Your training history, session logs, and performance metrics remain under your control.

3.2 Security Measures

  • Local database encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for any network communication
  • No third-party analytics SDKs with access to raw performance data
  • Payment processing via PCI-DSS compliant providers

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Payment processors — Stripe or equivalent, for subscription billing only
  • Legal obligations — If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Aggregated research — Anonymized, de-identified performance data may be used in published cognitive science research (Institutional tier participants are informed separately)

5. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Deletion — Request complete deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Export your session data in a standard format
  • Opt-out — Disable notifications, analytics, or cloud sync at any time via Settings
  • Correction — Update or correct your account information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mindylabs.com.

6. Children's Privacy

AKROS is designed for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us immediately for removal.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification. The Effective Date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
