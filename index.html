Operating Parameters

Is this a "brain training" game?
No. Most commercial brain games focus on entertainment and superficial engagement. AKROS focuses strictly on measurement and progressive resistance. It is a clinical-grade, performance-tracking utility designed to apply intense mental load and track your adaptation over time. It is not designed to be fun; it is designed to measure your baseline accurately.
How often should I run the protocols?
For optimal neurological adaptation, we recommend daily exposure. A standard session takes approximately 5-7 minutes. Consistency is more critical than duration. The app will track your operational streak and penalize inconsistent training patterns.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Total operational security. All performance metrics, baseline assessments, and session history are encrypted via AES-256 and stored exclusively on your local device hardware. We do not extract your data to cloud servers. No analytics. No tracking. You own your metrics.
