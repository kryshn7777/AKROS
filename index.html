METHODOLOGY

EVIDENCE-BASED PROTOCOLS

Every module is grounded in established neuroscience paradigms, adapted for measurable cognitive training with adaptive difficulty algorithms.

◆
N-BACK PARADIGM
The gold standard for working memory assessment. Continuous performance task that scales from N=1 to N=6.
Kirchner (1958) · Jaeggi et al. (2008)
◇
GO/NO-GO
Prefrontal inhibition via response suppression. The 75/25 ratio trains the anterior cingulate cortex to override prepotent motor responses.
Donders (1869) · Verbruggen & Logan (2008)
◈
STROOP INTERFERENCE
Selective attention under conflicting stimuli. Incongruent color-word trials strengthen top-down attentional control.
Stroop (1935) · MacLeod (1991)
◎
RSVP / ATTENTIONAL BLINK
Rapid Serial Visual Presentation at 250ms SOA reveals temporal bottlenecks in attention. Training reduces the "blink" window.
Raymond et al. (1992) · Chun & Potter (1995)
⬡
STAIRCASE ADAPTATION
Psychophysical staircase methods converge on individual performance thresholds. Difficulty calibrates in real-time.
Cornsweet (1962) · Levitt (1971)
⎔
SIGNAL DETECTION THEORY
Performance via d-prime separates true sensitivity from response bias. How psychophysics labs quantify perception — now in your pocket.
Green & Swets (1966) · Macmillan & Creelman (2005)
SUPPORT

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Does this actually improve cognitive performance?▾
Every module is based on peer-reviewed neuroscience paradigms with demonstrated transfer effects. N-Back training (Jaeggi et al., 2008) and Go/No-Go inhibition protocols have robust evidence for working memory and impulse control improvements respectively.
How is this different from brain training games?▾
AKROS uses psychophysical staircase algorithms and Signal Detection Theory (d-prime) scoring — the same methodologies used in research laboratories. No gamification, no fake scores. Your metrics are real and clinically meaningful.
What does a typical training session look like?▾
Sessions range from 7 minutes (Fog Reset primer) to 45 minutes (Endurance blocks). The adaptive load algorithm ensures every session trains at your cognitive threshold — challenging enough to trigger adaptation, not so hard it triggers frustration.
Is my data private?▾
All cognitive data stays on-device. No accounts required. No data leaves your phone. Your cognitive profile is yours alone.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?▾
Yes. Cancel anytime through Google Play. You retain Pro access until the end of your billing period. Your training data and progress are never deleted.
